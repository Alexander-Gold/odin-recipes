Alex's Chicken Paprikash

Hungarian chicken Paprikash that I usually make for Beth on her birthday. It's one of her favorite meals and it pairs well with a bed of rice and fresh green beans.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
