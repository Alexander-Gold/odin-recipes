Alex's Drumsticks

Delicious chicken drumsticks that Beth and I make just about every week together.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs chicken drumsticks
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp parsley flakes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425f
  2. Combine 2lbs chicken, olive oil, and all ingredients in to large mixing bowl.
  3. Cover bowl with cling film and shake until all ingredients are covering chicken evenly.
  4. Line large baking pan with aluminum foil and place chicken in pan
  5. Bake chicken for 30 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 165f
