Alex's Goulash

Delicious hungarian Goulash that I make on special occasions. Usually accompanied with spatzle dumplings, mashed potatos, and oven roasted green beans.

Ingredients

  • 2-3lbs stew beef
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2-3 tbsp paprika
  • 1 large white onion chopped
  • 3-4 whole cloves of garlic
  • 3 whole peeled tomatoes
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Using large pot heat olive oil and sautee onions until they become translucent.
  2. Salt and pepper beef and add it to the pot and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Fill pot just enough to cover beef with water.
  4. Add remaining ingredients and alow to simmer for 2-3 hours until beef becomes tender enough to fall apart.
